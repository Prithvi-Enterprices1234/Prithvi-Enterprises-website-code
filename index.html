About Prithvi Enterprises

Established in 1990 in Nashik, Maharashtra, we are a leading manufacturer specializing in premium modular kitchens, custom wooden furniture, and innovative interior design solutions.

Our state-of-the-art 4000 sq ft production facility in MIDC Ambad operates with cutting-edge technology and traditional craftsmanship, producing up to 1500 sq ft of wooden plywood daily.

Under the leadership of Mr. Rajeshwar Sharma, we continuously innovate with Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) designs, creating stunning cladding, sun filters, and pergolas that meet modern architectural demands.
